Disclaimer & Data Notes

This dashboard presents exploratory analysis of NYC's public land inventory as of PLUTO February 2026. Data includes 10,502 city-owned parcels across five boroughs. Potential unit estimates assume medium density development (14 units per acre) and are for planning purposes only. Flood zone exposure reflects FEMA 100-year floodplain data. Development costs are illustrative and based on comparable NYC projects; actual per-unit costs will vary by location, parcel characteristics, and market conditions. Acquisition sources represent historical patterns and do not guarantee future land availability. Timeline projections reflect optimistic planning scenarios and are subject to policy, funding, and community engagement outcomes. This analysis does not constitute a commitment to specific development or policy direction. For official public land policy information, consult NYC Department of City Planning (DCP), NYC Housing & Development Administration (HDA), or NYC Economic Development Corporation (NYCEDC).
